Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2025

Important: Nail Turn Manager is designed with your privacy in mind. Unlike many apps, we do not collect any personal data. All your information stays on your device.

1. Data Collection

Nail Turn Manager operates with a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • We do not collect personal information like names or email addresses
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your device's contacts, photos, or other apps
  • No usage data is sent to our servers

2. Local Data Storage

All app data including:

  • Your salon management records
  • App settings and preferences

...are stored only on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up or share it.

3. Data Usage

The app accesses your stored data only to:

  • Display your information when using the app
  • Generate reports and analytics
  • Provide the core functionality of the app

4. No Third-Party Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to share with third parties. We don't use any analytics or advertising services that would require data sharing.

5. Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data can be deleted by uninstalling the app
  • No accounts means no personal information to manage
  • You decide if/when to create backups

6. Security

While your data stays on your device, we recommend:

  • Using your device's security features (passcodes, biometrics)
  • Regularly updating your device's operating system
  • Being cautious when creating manual backups

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we'll:

  • Update this policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Notify users through the app if the changes are significant
  • Never reduce your privacy rights without explicit consent
